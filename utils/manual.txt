MOVEMENT:
	1. Use Up, Down, Left, and Right keyboard buttons to move player.
TASKS:
	1. Collect all coins before running out of time.
EXPLOSIVES:
	1. Pick up explosive by moving above it, YOU CAN ONLY PICK UP ONE EXPLOSIVE AT A TIME.
	2. Plot all explosive right next to the stone.
	3. Use the Plot Bomb button to plot a bomb.
	4. Bomb is placed at your current location.
	5. You only have one detonator for the explosives, so plant all the bombs and then detonate them.
  	6. Use the Detonator button to detonate the explosives.
	7. Some of the bombs are traps if they get you lose and if you run out of time you lose.
